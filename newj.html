The Kogan Guarantee promises that for each and every order on Kogan.com, you will get what you ordered, and it will be as described. If not we will:

  1. Ensure you receive the product you ordered, or if we can’t do that

If you don’t receive the products you ordered, or they are not as described, we will solve it for you. The easy steps are:

  1. Login to your Kogan.com Account that made the order
  2. Go to your Order History and select the order you need help with

For products sold by Kogan, we will take care of it from there and get back to you within 48 hours.
